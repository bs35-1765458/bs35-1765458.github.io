Services

Why Choose Us?

Care Advices

Each pet gets individually priced, based on the size of your pet, quality of their fur, and time estimated to complete the groom.

Customer Supports

Dedication to service is why our practice has become so well known and respected across the dog groming industry.

Emergency Services

We provide 24/7 emergency services to all pets in out facility to ensure safety during their stay with us.

Veterinary Help

Our ability to listen and communicate well with dogs to make sure we understand their health concerns and how to make the best choices.

Dog Walking

Private walks are wonderful for younger dogs that are still more interested in playing than walking in a small group, elderly dogs that need to take it nice and slow, or dogs working on overcoming fear aggression issues. When handling your pup’s private walks we will be more than happy to feed your pup and help reinforce any training you and your pup might be working on.

Pet Daycare

Professionally trained team will love and care for your pup like they are our own. You can trust your dog will come home overjoyed – and most likely overtired – from all of the excitement of the day. Similar to a child’s daycare, we provide recurring daycare for your dog, providing safe socialization, education and exercise. We will provide progress reports through report cards and conversations with you as a caring pet parent.

Pet Grooming

We offer a wide variety of services ranging from Nail Trims, Bathing, and Full Service Grooming. All pricing varies by breed, size, and current coat condition so it is always best to visit the Salon to speak with one of our professional groomers for an accurate estimate. We want your pet to not only look their best, but feel their best. Each and every groomer in our company has a genuine love for pets and we cater to each animal’s individual needs.
